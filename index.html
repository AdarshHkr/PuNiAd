Feature Matrix

Built with Professional Integrity

PuNiAd is designed to work completely offline, allowing you to track your habits anywhere, with smart cloud backup when you go online.

Distraction-Free Video Player

Play study videos directly from your local files. The app automatically structures them into organized subjects and chapters, allowing you to watch and track your progress completely offline.

Interactive Notes & Screenshots

Take structured notes during lectures. Tap a single button to instantly capture a question or answer snapshot into your notes. Scroll back through your timestamped review feed with inline lecture captures—no separate app or gallery needed.

Smart Meal Breakdown

Log meals easily. Gemini AI analyzes each ingredient separately—identifying macros, calorie density, and portion balance automatically. A built-in offline calculator scales saved meals instantly without any API call.

Adaptive Daily Goals

Set daily targets for study, hydration, and journaling. Adjust your goals at any time—your past achievement history is preserved correctly so you can see your consistency grow.

Routine & Hydration Tracker

Keep a personal log of your daily workouts and water intake. Calculated parameters adapt dynamically to help you reach your custom targets.

Reflective Journal & Tasks

Reflect on your daily wins, challenges, and thoughts. Keep checklists of daily tasks to clear your mind and stay focused on what matters.

Smart Daily Advice

Synthesizes your diet, hydration, and exercise into clear, personalized suggestions on your dashboard to help you recover and improve daily.

Privacy-Protected Sync

Choose exactly what information is backed up to the cloud. Keeps your data synchronized in both directions between all your devices with zero duplicate records.
